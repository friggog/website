Cambridge Glycoscience is making sugar substitutes that work in food - so you can have your cake and eat it too!

WHAT WE DO

Cambridge Glycoscience develops functional food ingredients for a low-sugar, high-fibre, all-around healthy food system. Overconsumption of sugar is one of the world's greatest public health crises - causing heart disease, obesity, diabetes and more early deaths in the US than any other factor. The most impactful way of resolving the problem is through product reformulation of food and beverages. Our products are...

DELICIOUS

Our ingredients allow 1:1 sugar replacement in foods, such as bakery products and snacks. They do this by replicating the various roles that sugar serves in food besides sweetness, such as texture, browning and caramelisation - with no aftertaste.

SUGAR-FREE & LOW GLYCAEMIC INDEX

By replacing sugar with our ingredient, your food products can become low GI and diabetic-friendly.

LOW-CALORIE

Our ingredients help you feel full without the calories of a high-sugar snack, helping you stay in great shape.

NON-ARTIFICIAL & NON-GMO

Our ingredients are plant-based, non-GMO products and are sourced sustainably.

HIGH FIBER

High-fiber diets are important for gut microbial health. Our ingredients have prebiotic effects, whilst being well-tolerated.

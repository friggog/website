Join us as we dance through the shortest night into the longest day, while the sun stands still. Witness the transformation of the beautiful grounds of Trinity Hall under the light of the midnight sun. Experience the enticing array of food, drink, music and entertainment we have to offer.

With installations that will bring the solstice festivities to life, and the unmissable friendly atmosphere that characterises us, Trinity Hall's 2018 June Event is the ideal place to celebrate the summer solstice.

We have more exciting announcements on the way which we cannot wait to share with you. As the sun marks its highest point in the sky come to Solstice, Trinity Hall's 2018 June Event, to mark the highest point in your May Week.

We look forward to welcoming you to Trinity Hall on Wednesday 20th June 2018, the eve of the summer solstice.

CHARITIES

KASANKA TRUST

The Kasanka Trust is a non-profit charitable institution responsible for the Park management, community development and tourism of Kasanka National Park, Lavushi Manda National Park and Shoebill Island Camp in the Bangweulu Wetlands.

JIMMY'S

For over 20 years, Jimmy's has been providing help for the homeless in Cambridge. Working with the community to deliver emergency accommodation and supported housing for those who would otherwise be homeless.
